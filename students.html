Welcome! If you are interested in joining my group at the University of Ottawa, please read the information below.

Research Areas: My group works on trustworthy machine learning, data privacy, security, and related topics. I am especially interested in data poisoning, machine unlearning, and building robust and ethical AI systems. I am also keenly interested in the areas of AI policy, auditing, and governance.

Who Should Apply? My home department is the School of Engineering Design and Teaching Innovation at the University of Ottawa, and I conduct interdisciplinary research at the intersection of computer science, engineering, policy, and society. I welcome applications from students with backgrounds in computer science, engineering, mathematics, as well as those from social science or law. If you are passionate about research and eager to learn, I encourage you to reach out!

Opportunities: Fully funded PhD and MSc positions (Fall 2026 and beyond), undergraduate research opportunities, and visiting students.

How to Apply: If you are interested in joining my group, please fill out the following form: Application Form. I will review your submission and contact you if there is a potential fit. I will start reviewing applications in late July and conducting zoom interviews early August.

Note: (1) I cannot officially proceed with your application until September 2025. (2) If you are already a student at University of Ottawa, please email me directly. (3) This page will be updated when the instruction for formal application is received from the University of Ottawa.
